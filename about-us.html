Types of Flags:

  1. Outdoor Flags: These are large, durable flags that are perfect for flying high on flagpoles outside homes, schools, government buildings, and offices. Made from weather-resistant materials, our outdoor flags can withstand harsh conditions and remain vibrant and sturdy.
  2. Indoor Flags: These are designed for indoor display, made from soft, premium fabric that looks elegant in any setting. These flags are ideal for offices, homes, or as decor in patriotic events.
  3. Table Flags: These small-sized flags are perfect for desks and tables. They are often used in conferences, official events, and as symbols of respect and pride in corporate settings.
  4. Handheld Flags: Perfect for rallies, parades, and public celebrations, these flags are light, portable, and easy to carry, making them ideal for spreading the patriotic spirit during events like Independence Day and Republic Day.
  5. Customized Flags: In addition to the standard tirangaprimes, we also offer customized options for various purposes. Whether it’s for corporate events, government functions, or special commemorative occasions, we can create flags to meet specific needs and preferences.
